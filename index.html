About Our Hotel

THE HISTORY OF OUR HOTEL

The Paradise Inn Hotel is a heritage, five-star, luxury hotel built in the Saracenic Revival style in the Colaba area of Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, It has been considered one of the finest hotels in the East.the hotel has 560 rooms and 44 suites and is considered the flagship property of the group; it employs 1,600 staff. The hotel is made up of two different buildings.The hotel has a long and distinguished history, having received many notable guests, from presidents to captains of industry and stars of show business.During World War I, the hotel was converted into a military hospital with 600 beds.
